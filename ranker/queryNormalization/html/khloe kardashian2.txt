tristan thompson hung out with a fifth woman during khloé kardashian's pregnancy
photos seem to show another woman leaving the basketball player's hotel.
by
amy mackelden
apr 14, 2018
getty images
following the birth of khloé kardashian's first child this week, the cheating scandal involving tristan thompson, the father of her baby, continues.

according to the daily mail, a fifth woman was spotted hanging out with, and possibly having an affair with tristan thompson, while his girlfriend, khloé, was pregnant. despite multiple claims that kardashian has forgiven the basketball player, the accusations about his behavior are not dying down. as the daily mail reports, the fifth woman that thompson allegedly had an affair with is called "tania, and tristan was first spotted with her back in november, and last seen with her less than a month ago in new york." the daily mail has photos of the pair together, and leaving multiple locations at similar times.the new report tracks thompson's movements over the past few months, and places him with tania on several occasions. apparently they were first seen together leaving the four seasons downtown, manhattan, in november 2017, which is the same hotel that blair, one of the other women thompson has been linked to, was also seen at.

over the past week, multiple reports have linked thompson to a number of women throughout kardashian's pregnancy. tmz shared a video reportedly from october 2017, in which thompson could be seen kissing two women, one of whom revealed to the website that she was led to believe that thompson and kardashian's relationship was "casual."an "insider" also told people this week that "tristan has been consistently cheating on khloé. he’s a serial cheater. and there will be more women to come out of the woodwork."
